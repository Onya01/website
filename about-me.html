My name is Uchenna and living the simple life saved my life

I used to be a pack rat. I had so many things that I thought that I learnd, but the truth was I couldn't even appraciate any of it because there too much.

How I turn things around

I was on the verge of a brokedown when I got rid of everything. I literally put 90% of my possions in the garbage, sold my place and moved into smaller apartment, and I quit my job.

It wasn't easy at all, but everything around me around me in such a bad place, I didn't know what else to do. And it worked.

How I live the simple life

Now that I'm living a simple life, things are so much better. I'm less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enjoy.

With how much it turned my life around, I felt I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from living a simple life as well, which is why I started this site
